Services

Remodeling

We love the challenge of fundamentally reworking your space. Designing a new, more functional footprint to better utilize and enjoy this area of your home. To remodel is to add wholly new structure to a space and to rework a floor plan. More comprehensive and often more expensive than a renovation, remodeling can add significant value to a home, as well as improve utility for the residents.

Renovation

Essentially a facelift for your space. Updating and modernizing the existing footprint. For bathrooms, this often entails replacing an older style standing shower and soaking tub combo with a custom tile full size shower, new vanity, tile floors, etc... For kitchens, new cabinets, countertops, fixtures and appliances.
